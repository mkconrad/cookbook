california carbonara

i think this is from cpk?

serves 2

ingredients

  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 4-6 strips of bacon or bbq chicken breast, diced
  • 6 shallots or 1 leek, sliced crosswise
  • 1-2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 fresh/dried hot pepper, optional
  • 6 sun-dried tomatoes (marinated in oil), sliced thinly
  • 2 tbsp pine nuts
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup half & half
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 8 oz angel hair, egg, spinach pasta
  • 1/4 cup italian parsley, stems removed & chopped
  • herbs: basil, thyme, rosemary, oregano (to taste) optional

directions

  1. cook pasta to according to directions
  2. in frying pan over med-high heat, cook bacon to medium crispiness. when cooked, drain the fat and chop into half-inch slices. reserve
  3. over med-high heat melt butter in a saute pan. add shallots (or leeks), garlic, hot peppers, and pine nuts and saute for 5 mins or until brown
  4. reduce heat of saute pan to med-low heat and add sun-dried tomatoes, bacon, herbs (not parsley), and chicken optional for 2 mins
  5. in a bowl, beat eggs, cream, black pepper, and all but 1 tbsp of parm
  6. remove saute pan from burner and leave contents in pan
  7. drain pasta, then place in saute pan
  8. pour egg mixture over pasta and sauteed contents. add parsley. toss for ~30 secs allowing the egg mixture to coat the pasta
  9. serve on warmed plates/bowls
