panera mac & cheese

need i say more?

serves ##

ingredients

  • 4 cups heavy cream
  • 1 stick unsalted butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 lb/16 oz shell pasta
  • 14-16oz [vermont] white cheddar, grated
  • 1/2 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp white pepper
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tsp mustard powder, optional

directions

  1. put pot of water on stove top, let come to a boil, add pasta & a hefty pinch of salt, boil for 10-15 min
  2. simmer your heavy cream on stove top - before adding it to the rest of your ingredients (simmer on low heat NOT boil)
  3. in a sauce pan, melt down your butter. add in flour & cook for 4-6 min or until your flour cooks into a blondish golden color
  4. Add in your warmed heavy cream , seasonings, and cheese listed above. Mix everything together well & add in your pasta (all on low heat)
  5. Simmer together until it’s a thicker consistency & not too runny:,) (the longer you simmer heavy cream the thicker it will become! You’re in charge of the consistency:,)
  6. give it a taste & see if you’d like to add anymore of whatever seasoning & stir again
  7. Serve however you’d like, and you’re done!

    8. note: if you find your seasoning clumping together in your sauce - an immersion blender will do the trick :,) perfectly smooth sauce after using it
