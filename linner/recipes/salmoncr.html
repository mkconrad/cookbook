salmon crispy rice

honestly im making this shit up as i go...i measure with love lmao, fuck it uppppp

serves 2

ingredients

  • sushi grade salmon/sashimi
  • kewpie mayo
  • siracha
  • avocado
  • white rice, cooked
  • green onion
  • rice wine vinegar
  • granulated sugar
  • salt
  • masago, optional

directions

  1. mix together cooked rice, rice wine vinegar, sugar and salt
  2. put plastic wrap in a square tupperware and flatten rice on top of it
  3. let chill in the fridge for at least an hour
  4. once firm, cut into small rectangles and fry in oil until golden or crispy
  5. cut salmon and mix in 2:1 ratio of kewpie mayo to siracha (w)ill become a light orange color) and mix in handful of green onion
  6. cut avocado and smush it in a bowl
  7. to assemble, layer rice, then avocado, and lastly the salmon
  8. top with scallions and enjoy!
