spring rolls & peanut sauce

i was about to eat the peanut sauce from the pot. literally the best thing i've ever had.

serves as many people as you want :)

ingredients

spring roll:

  • spring roll wrappers
  • vermicelli rice noodles
  • shrimp
  • lettuce
  • pork (loin, shoulder or belly)
  • other fillings
    • cucumber
    • carrot
    • mint

peanut sauce:

  • hoisin sauce
  • water
  • peanut butter, chunky
  • cornstartch
  • garlic, minced & 3-4 cloves

directions

  1. boil water and cook pork for ~20 mins
  2. boil water and cook shrimp until they turn color
  3. immediately put into cold/ice water so they do not shrink
  4. boil even more water, and cook the vermicelli noodles until soft ~3 mins but just eyeball it
  5. for the peanut sauce put 3 heaping spoonfuls of hoisin, 12 spoons of water, and some cornstarch into a pot and simmer. add the peanut butter and garlic and continue to mix and sit on medium-low heat. cook to taste.
