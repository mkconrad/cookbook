aglio e olio

serves ##

ingredients

  • salt
  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 8 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley, minced
  • 1 cup parmesan, grated

directions

  1. cook pasta and save 1 1/2 cup of pasta water
  2. heat olive oil over medium heat
  3. add garlic and cook for 2 mins
  4. add red pepper flakes (30 secs)
  5. add pasta water and bring to boil
  6. add 1 tsp salt and simmer
  7. add pasta and toss
  8. turn off heat, add parsley & parmesan
  9. allow pasta to rest for 5 mins
