olive garden alfredo pasta

make with some sexy sexy bread mmmm

serves 2-3

ingredients

  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) butter
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/8 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
  • 12 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1/4 cup parmesan, grated

directions

  1. melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat
  2. add cream, garlic powder, & pepper and simmer for 10-12 mins or until thick
  3. boil water and cook pasta according to box
  4. when sauce has reached the desired consistency, stir in parmesan
  5. drain pasta, put on dishes and top with the alfredo sauce
