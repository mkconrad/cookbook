bolognese pasta

serves:

meg's rating:

ingredients:

  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1 lb ground sirloin
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1 1/4 cup red wine, divided
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 3/4 dried pasta, shells or orecchiette
  • 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated parm

directions!

  1. bring large pot of water to a boil & add a tbsp of salt, splash of oil, & the pasta, cook according to the box
  2. heat 2 tbsp of olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat
  3. add ground sirloin & cook, crumbling the meat with a wooden spoon, (5-7 mins), until meat has started to brown
  4. stir in garlic, oregano, & red pepper flakes and cook for 1 more min
  5. pour 1 cup of wine into skillet and stir to scrape any browned bits
  6. add tomatoes, tomato paste, 1 tbsp salt, & 1 1/2 tsp pepper, stirring until combined
  7. bring to a boil, lower heat, and simmer for 10 mins
  8. for the sauce, add nutmeg, basil, cream, and the remaining 1/4 cup wine to the sauce
  9. simmer for 8-10 mins, stirring occasionally until thickened
  10. add pasta to sauce and 1/2 cup parmesan and tosss

enjoy!
